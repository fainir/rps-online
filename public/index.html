How to play

  • Each piece is secretly Rock ✊, Paper ✋ or Scissors ✌️ — you see yours, your rival sees blanks.
  • Move one piece one square (up/down/left/right) per turn.
  • Move onto a rival to attack: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock.
  • A tie starts a live throw-off — both players pick a hand until someone wins.
  • The Trap 💣 destroys any attacker. Your Flag 🚩 can't move.
  • Win by capturing the rival's flag, or leaving them with no legal move.
